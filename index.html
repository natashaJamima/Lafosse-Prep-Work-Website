About Me

As I write this I am 25 years young and currently rocking a short bob. I'm best known for my random outbreaks into song and getting these songs stuck in your head, even if you don't know them. I love watching new movies, so currently December is ruining the vibe because the cringy Christmas rom-coms are not for me - unless I'm with the girls, of course. Aside from my visual entertainment, music is my go to for every mood and situation.
